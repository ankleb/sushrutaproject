\section{Literature}

\emph{svasthavαΙ¦tta} in the vulgate.\fvolcite{IA}[330--331]{meul-hist} There,

Hara (1995) and P.V. Sharma (1985e: 52-53).\fvolcite{IB}[10]{meul-hist}
